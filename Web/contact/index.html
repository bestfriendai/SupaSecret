Before You Contact Us

Please check our Help & Support page first - you might find your answer there!

For Technical Support, Please Include:

  • Your username (if applicable)
  • Device type (iPhone 14, Samsung Galaxy S23, etc.)
  • Operating system version (iOS 17, Android 13, etc.)
  • App version (found in Settings → About)
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings (if relevant)
  • Steps to reproduce the problem

For Account Issues:

  • Your username
  • Email associated with the account (if any)
  • Description of the issue
  • When the issue started

For Content Reports:

  • Use the in-app "Report" feature for fastest response
  • Include the confession ID or link
  • Explain why the content violates our Terms of Service

Crisis Resources

🆘 If You're in Crisis

Toxic Confessions is not a crisis service. If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please reach out to professional help immediately:

  • 🇺🇸 US: Call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline)
  • 💬 Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • 🌍 International: iasp.info/resources/Crisis_Centres
  • 🚨 Emergency: Call 911 (US) or your local emergency number
